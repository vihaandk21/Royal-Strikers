About Royal Strikers

Welcome to Royal Strikers! We are a passionate sports community dedicated to promoting Throwball and Volleyball through exciting tournaments and competitive events. Our goal is to create opportunities for players to showcase their talent, build confidence, and grow through experience. Though we're just getting started, we believe in dreaming big, working hard, and never giving up. Together, we're building a future where Royal Strikers becomes a name recognized for sportsmanship, unity, and excellence. Play with passion. Compete with pride. Grow with Royal Strikers. 🚀🏆

For our upcoming tournament, the entry fee is ₹600 for the School Level (no cash prize) and ₹1,000 for the College Level (which includes a cash prize). Full payment is required upon registration. We will be providing tournament lunch for all participating teams, and every participant will receive an official certificate. All matches will be played using official Cosco brand volleyballs. Please note that since this is an outdoor event, there is a possibility of rain, and our bad weather contingency policy will apply.
